How to Practice Effectively

1
Pattern-first, not problem-first Learn the pattern → solve 3-5 problems → move to the next.
2
Time yourself 25 min per problem. Stuck at 15 min → read the approach (not code), retry.
3
Explain out loud If you can't explain it in English, you'll freeze in the interview.
4
Spaced repetition Solve → wait 3 days → re-solve without looking.
5
Track progress Use the checkboxes on each list page. Target: 3 per day, 6-8 weeks.
