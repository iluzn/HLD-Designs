<h1>Maximum Swap</h1>

<p>Given a non-negative integer <code>n</code>, you may swap two digits at most once to get the maximum valued number. Return that maximum value.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  n = 2736
Output: 7236
Explanation: Swap 2 and 7.</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  n = 9973
Output: 9973</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= n &lt;= 10^8</li></ul>
