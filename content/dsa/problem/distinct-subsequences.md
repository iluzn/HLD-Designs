<h1>Distinct Subsequences</h1>
<span class="lc-diff hard">Hard</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">String</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>Given two strings <code>s</code> and <code>t</code>, return the number of distinct subsequences of <code>s</code> that equal <code>t</code>. The answer fits in a 32-bit integer.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  s = "rabbbit", t = "rabbit"
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  s = "babgbag", t = "bag"
Output: 5</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= s.length, t.length &lt;= 1000</li><li>Inputs consist of English letters.</li></ul>
