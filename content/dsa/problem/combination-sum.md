<h1>Combination Sum</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Backtracking</span></div></details>
<p>Given distinct positive integers <code>candidates</code> and a <code>target</code>, return all unique combinations that sum to target. A candidate may be reused unlimited times.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  candidates = [2,3,6,7], target = 7
Output: [[2,2,3],[7]]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  candidates = [2,3,5], target = 8
Output: [[2,2,2,2],[2,3,3],[3,5]]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= candidates.length &lt;= 30</li><li>All candidates are distinct positive integers.</li><li>1 &lt;= target &lt;= 40</li></ul>
