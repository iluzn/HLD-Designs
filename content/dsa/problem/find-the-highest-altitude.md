<h1>Find the Highest Altitude</h1>
<span class="lc-diff easy">Easy</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Prefix Sum</span></div></details>
<p>A biker starts at altitude 0. <code>gain[i]</code> is the net gain between point i and i+1. Return the highest altitude reached.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  gain = [-5,1,5,0,-7]
Output: 1</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  gain = [-4,-3,-2,-1,4,3,2]
Output: 0</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= gain.length &lt;= 100</li><li>-100 &lt;= gain[i] &lt;= 100</li></ul>
