<h1>Logger Rate Limiter</h1>
<span class="lc-diff easy">Easy</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Hash Table</span><span class="lc-tag">Design</span><span class="lc-tag">Data Stream</span></div></details>
<p>Design a logger that limits each unique message to being printed at most once every 10 seconds. Implement <code>Logger()</code> and <code>shouldPrintMessage(timestamp, message)</code>, which returns <code>true</code> if the message should be printed at the given <code>timestamp</code> (in seconds), and <code>false</code> otherwise. A message printed at time <code>t</code> may next be printed at <code>t + 10</code> or later.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  operations = ["Logger","shouldPrintMessage","shouldPrintMessage","shouldPrintMessage","shouldPrintMessage","shouldPrintMessage","shouldPrintMessage"], values = [[],[1,"foo"],[2,"bar"],[3,"foo"],[8,"bar"],[10,"foo"],[11,"foo"]]
Output: [null,true,true,false,false,false,true]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= timestamp &lt;= 10^9</li><li>Every timestamp is greater than or equal to the previous.</li><li>At most 10^4 calls.</li></ul>
