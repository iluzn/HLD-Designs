<h1>Design Hit Counter</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Queue</span><span class="lc-tag">Design</span><span class="lc-tag">Binary Search</span><span class="lc-tag">Data Stream</span></div></details>
<p>Design a hit counter that counts hits received in the past 5 minutes (300 seconds). Implement <code>HitCounter()</code>, <code>hit(timestamp)</code> which records a hit at the given <code>timestamp</code> (in seconds), and <code>getHits(timestamp)</code> which returns the number of hits in the past 300 seconds — that is, hits with a recorded time in the range <code>(timestamp - 300, timestamp]</code>. Calls are made in non-decreasing time order.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  operations = ["HitCounter","hit","hit","hit","getHits","hit","getHits","getHits"], values = [[],[1],[2],[3],[4],[300],[300],[301]]
Output: [null,null,null,null,3,null,4,3]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= timestamp &lt;= 2*10^9</li><li>All calls are made with non-decreasing timestamps.</li><li>At most 300 calls per getHits window in the basic version.</li></ul>
