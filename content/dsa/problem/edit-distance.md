<h1>Edit Distance</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">String</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>Given two strings <code>word1</code> and <code>word2</code>, return the minimum number of operations (insert, delete, or replace a character) to convert word1 into word2.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  word1 = "horse", word2 = "ros"
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  word1 = "intention", word2 = "execution"
Output: 5</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= word1.length, word2.length &lt;= 500</li><li>Inputs consist of lowercase English letters.</li></ul>
