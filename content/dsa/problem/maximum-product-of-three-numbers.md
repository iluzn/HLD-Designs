<h1>Maximum Product of Three Numbers</h1>

<p>Given an integer array <code>nums</code>, find three numbers whose product is maximum and return that product.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [1,2,3]
Output: 6</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [-4,-3,-2,-1,60]
Output: 720</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>3 &lt;= nums.length &lt;= 10^4</li><li>-1000 &lt;= nums[i] &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
