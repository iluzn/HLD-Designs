<h1>Car Fleet</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Stack</span><span class="lc-tag">Sorting</span><span class="lc-tag">Monotonic Stack</span></div></details>
<p>Cars head to a destination at <code>target</code>. Car <code>i</code> starts at <code>position[i]</code> with constant <code>speed[i]</code>; a faster car catches up to a slower one ahead and then travels as a fleet at the slower speed. Return the number of car fleets that arrive.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  target = 12, position = [10,8,0,5,3], speed = [2,4,1,1,3]
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  target = 10, position = [3], speed = [3]
Output: 1</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= n &lt;= 10^5</li><li>0 &lt; target &lt;= 10^6</li><li>All positions are unique.</li></ul>
