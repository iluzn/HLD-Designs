<h1>Boats to Save People</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Greedy</span><span class="lc-tag">Two Pointers</span><span class="lc-tag">Sorting</span></div></details>
<p>You are given weights <code>nums</code> and a boat capacity <code>target</code>. Each boat carries at most two people whose combined weight is at most <code>target</code>. Return the minimum number of boats to carry everyone.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  people = [1,2], limit = 3
Output: 1</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  people = [3,2,2,1], limit = 3
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 3</h2><pre>Input:  people = [3,5,3,4], limit = 5
Output: 4</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= nums.length &lt;= 5*10^4</li><li>1 &lt;= nums[i] &lt;= target &lt;= 3*10^4</li></ul>
