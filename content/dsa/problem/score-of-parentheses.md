<h1>Score of Parentheses</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">String</span><span class="lc-tag">Stack</span></div></details>
<p>Given a balanced parentheses string <code>s</code>, compute its score: <code>()</code> is 1, <code>AB</code> is A+B, and <code>(A)</code> is 2*A.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  s = "()"
Output: 1</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  s = "(())"
Output: 2</pre>
<h2>Example 3</h2><pre>Input:  s = "()()"
Output: 2</pre>
<h2>Example 4</h2><pre>Input:  s = "(()(()))"
Output: 6</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>2 &lt;= s.length &lt;= 50</li><li>s is a balanced parentheses string.</li></ul>
