<h1>House Robber II</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>Same as House Robber, but the houses are arranged in a <strong>circle</strong> — the first and last are adjacent. Return the maximum you can rob.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [2,3,2]
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [1,2,3,1]
Output: 4</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= nums.length &lt;= 100</li><li>0 &lt;= nums[i] &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
