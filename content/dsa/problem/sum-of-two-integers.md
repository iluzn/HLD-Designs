<h1>Sum of Two Integers</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Math</span><span class="lc-tag">Bit Manipulation</span></div></details>
<p>Given two integers <code>a</code> and <code>b</code>, return their sum <strong>without</strong> using the operators <code>+</code> or <code>-</code>.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  a = 1, b = 2
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  a = 2, b = 3
Output: 5</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>-1000 &lt;= a, b &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
