<h1>Symmetric Tree</h1>
<span class="lc-diff easy">Easy</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Tree</span><span class="lc-tag">DFS</span><span class="lc-tag">BFS</span></div></details>
<p>Given the <code>root</code> of a binary tree, check whether it is a mirror of itself (symmetric around its center).</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  root = [1,2,2,3,4,4,3]
Output: true</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  root = [1,2,2,null,3,null,3]
Output: false</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= number of nodes &lt;= 1000</li><li>-100 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 100</li></ul>
