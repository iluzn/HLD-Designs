<h1>Maximum Product of Word Lengths</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">String</span><span class="lc-tag">Bit Manipulation</span></div></details>
<p>Given an array of strings <code>words</code>, return the maximum value of <code>length(words[i]) * length(words[j])</code> where the two words share no common letters. If no such pair exists, return 0.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  words = ["abcw","baz","foo","bar","xtfn","abcdef"]
Output: 16
Explanation: "abcw" and "xtfn" share no letters: 4 * 4 = 16.</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  words = ["a","aa","aaa","aaaa"]
Output: 0</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>2 &lt;= words.length &lt;= 1000</li><li>1 &lt;= words[i].length &lt;= 1000</li><li>words[i] consists of lowercase English letters.</li></ul>
