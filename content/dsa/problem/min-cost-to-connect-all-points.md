<h1>Min Cost to Connect All Points</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Graph</span><span class="lc-tag">Minimum Spanning Tree</span><span class="lc-tag">Heap</span></div></details>
<p>You are given <code>points</code> where <code>points[i] = [x, y]</code>. The cost to connect two points is their Manhattan distance. Return the minimum cost to connect all points so every pair is reachable. (Input rows are the points.)</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  points = [[0,0],[2,2],[3,10],[5,2],[7,0]]
Output: 20</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  points = [[3,12],[-2,5],[-4,1]]
Output: 18</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= points.length &lt;= 1000</li><li>-10^6 &lt;= xi, yi &lt;= 10^6</li></ul>
