<h1>Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock</h1>
<span class="lc-diff easy">Easy</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>You are given an array <code>prices</code> where <code>prices[i]</code> is the price of a stock on day <code>i</code>. Maximize profit by choosing one day to buy and a later day to sell. Return the max profit, or 0 if none.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [7,1,5,3,6,4]
Output: 5
Explanation: Buy at 1, sell at 6.</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [7,6,4,3,1]
Output: 0</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= prices.length &lt;= 10^5</li><li>0 &lt;= prices[i] &lt;= 10^4</li></ul>
