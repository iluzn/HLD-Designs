<h1>Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock with Cooldown</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>Given daily stock <code>prices</code>, maximize profit with as many transactions as you like, but after you sell you must cooldown one day before buying again. Return the max profit.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [1,2,3,0,2]
Output: 3
Explanation: buy, sell, cooldown, buy, sell.</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [1]
Output: 0</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= prices.length &lt;= 5000</li><li>0 &lt;= prices[i] &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
