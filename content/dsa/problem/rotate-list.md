<h1>Rotate List</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Linked List</span><span class="lc-tag">Two Pointers</span></div></details>
<p>Given the <code>head</code> of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by <code>k</code> places and return the new head.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2
Output: [4,5,1,2,3]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  head = [0,1,2], k = 4
Output: [2,0,1]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= number of nodes &lt;= 500</li><li>-100 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 100</li><li>0 &lt;= k &lt;= 2*10^9</li></ul>
