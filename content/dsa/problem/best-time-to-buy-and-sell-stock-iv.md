<h1>Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock IV</h1>
<span class="lc-diff hard">Hard</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>You are given an integer array <code>nums</code> where <code>nums[i]</code> is the price of a stock on day <code>i</code>, and an integer <code>k</code>. Return the maximum profit achievable with <strong>at most <code>k</code> transactions</strong>. You may not hold more than one share at a time.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [2,4,1], k = 2
Output: 2</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  nums = [3,2,6,5,0,3], k = 2
Output: 7</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= k &lt;= 100</li><li>1 &lt;= nums.length &lt;= 1000</li><li>0 &lt;= nums[i] &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
