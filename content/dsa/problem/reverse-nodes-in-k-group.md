<h1>Reverse Nodes in k-Group</h1>
<span class="lc-diff hard">Hard</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Linked List</span><span class="lc-tag">Recursion</span></div></details>
<p>Given the <code>head</code> of a linked list, reverse the nodes <code>k</code> at a time and return the modified list. Nodes left over (fewer than <code>k</code>) stay in their original order.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2
Output: [2,1,4,3,5]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 3
Output: [3,2,1,4,5]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= number of nodes &lt;= 5000</li><li>0 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 1000</li><li>1 &lt;= k &lt;= number of nodes</li></ul>
