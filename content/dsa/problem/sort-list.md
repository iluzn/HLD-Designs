<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  head = [4,2,1,3]
Output: [1,2,3,4]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  head = [-1,5,3,4,0]
Output: [-1,0,3,4,5]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= number of nodes &lt;= 5*10^4</li><li>-10^5 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 10^5</li></ul>
