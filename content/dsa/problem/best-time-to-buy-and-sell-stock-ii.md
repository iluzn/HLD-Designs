<h1>Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock II</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Greedy</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span></div></details>
<p>You are given an array <code>prices</code> where <code>prices[i]</code> is the price of a stock on day <code>i</code>. You may buy and sell as many times as you like (but hold at most one share at a time). Return the maximum profit.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [7,1,5,3,6,4]
Output: 7</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  prices = [1,2,3,4,5]
Output: 4</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= prices.length &lt;= 3*10^4</li><li>0 &lt;= prices[i] &lt;= 10^4</li></ul>
