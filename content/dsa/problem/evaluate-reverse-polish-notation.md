<h1>Evaluate Reverse Polish Notation</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Stack</span><span class="lc-tag">Math</span></div></details>
<p>Evaluate an arithmetic expression in Reverse Polish Notation given as a list of <code>tokens</code>. Valid operators are <code>+ - * /</code>; division truncates toward zero.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  tokens = ["2","1","+","3","*"]
Output: 9</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  tokens = ["4","13","5","/","+"]
Output: 6</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= tokens.length &lt;= 10^4</li><li>Tokens are integers or one of + - * /.</li></ul>
