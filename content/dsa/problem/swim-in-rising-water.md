<h1>Swim in Rising Water</h1>
<span class="lc-diff hard">Hard</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Graph</span><span class="lc-tag">Binary Search</span><span class="lc-tag">Heap</span><span class="lc-tag">Matrix</span></div></details>
<p>You are given an <code>n x n</code> grid where <code>grid[i][j]</code> is the elevation at that cell. At time <code>t</code> the water level is <code>t</code>, and you can move between two adjacent cells if both elevations are at most <code>t</code>. Starting at the top-left, return the least time to reach the bottom-right.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  grid = [[0,2],[1,3]]
Output: 3</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  grid = [[0,1,2,3,4],[24,23,22,21,5],[12,13,14,15,16],[11,17,18,19,20],[10,9,8,7,6]]
Output: 16</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= n &lt;= 50</li><li>grid holds a permutation of 0 .. n^2 - 1</li></ul>
