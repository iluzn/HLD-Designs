<h1>Dungeon Game</h1>
<span class="lc-diff hard">Hard</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Dynamic Programming</span><span class="lc-tag">Matrix</span></div></details>
<p>A knight starts at the top-left cell of <code>grid</code> and must reach the bottom-right cell, moving only right or down. Each cell adds its value to the knight&#39;s health (negative values are demons, positive are potions). The knight dies if health drops to <code>0</code> or below at any point. Return the minimum initial health needed to survive the journey.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  dungeon = [[-2,-3,3],[-5,-10,1],[10,30,-5]]
Output: 7</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  dungeon = [[0]]
Output: 1</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= rows, cols &lt;= 200</li><li>-1000 &lt;= grid[i][j] &lt;= 1000</li></ul>
