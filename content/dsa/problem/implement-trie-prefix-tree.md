<p>Implement a trie with <code>insert(word)</code>, <code>search(word)</code> (returns whether the exact word was inserted), and <code>startsWith(prefix)</code> (returns whether any inserted word has the given prefix).</p>

<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= word.length, prefix.length &lt;= 2000</li><li>Words consist of lowercase English letters.</li><li>At most 3*10^4 calls.</li></ul>
