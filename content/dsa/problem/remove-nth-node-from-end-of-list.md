<h1>Remove Nth Node From End of List</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>

<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2
Output: [1,2,3,5]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1], k = 1
Output: []</pre>
