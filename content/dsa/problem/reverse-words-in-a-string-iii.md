<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">String</span><span class="lc-tag">Two Pointers</span></div></details>
<p>Given a string <code>s</code> of words separated by single spaces, reverse the characters of each word while preserving word order and spacing.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  s = "Lets code"
Output: "steL edoc"</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  s = "God Ding"
Output: "doG gniD"</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= s.length &lt;= 5*10^4</li><li>Words are separated by single spaces with no leading/trailing spaces.</li></ul>
