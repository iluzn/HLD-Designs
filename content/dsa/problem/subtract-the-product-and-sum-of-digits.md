<p>Given an integer <code>n</code>, return the product of its digits minus the sum of its digits.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  n = 234
Output: 15
Explanation: product = 24, sum = 9, 24 - 9 = 15.</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  n = 4421
Output: 21</pre>
