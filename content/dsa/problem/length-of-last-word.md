<h1>Length of Last Word</h1>
<span class="lc-diff easy">Easy</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">String</span></div></details>
<p>Given a string <code>s</code> of words and spaces, return the length of the <strong>last</strong> word. A word is a maximal substring of non-space characters.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  s = "Hello World"
Output: 5</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  s = "   fly me   to   the moon  "
Output: 4</pre>
<h2>Example 3</h2><pre>Input:  s = "luffy is still joyboy"
Output: 6</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= s.length &lt;= 10^4</li><li>s consists of English letters and spaces, with at least one word.</li></ul>
