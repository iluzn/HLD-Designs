<h1>Surrounded Regions</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Graph</span><span class="lc-tag">DFS</span><span class="lc-tag">BFS</span><span class="lc-tag">Matrix</span></div></details>
<p>Given an <code>m x n</code> board of <code>'X'</code> and <code>'O'</code>, capture all regions of <code>'O'</code> that are fully surrounded by <code>'X'</code> (flip them to <code>'X'</code>). Any <code>'O'</code> connected to the border is safe. Return the resulting board.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  board = ["XXXX","XOOX","XXOX","XOXX"]
Output: ["XXXX","XXXX","XXXX","XOXX"]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= m, n &lt;= 200</li><li>board[i][j] is 'X' or 'O'.</li></ul>
