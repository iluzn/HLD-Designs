<h1>Koko Eating Bananas</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Array</span><span class="lc-tag">Binary Search</span></div></details>
<p>Koko has piles of bananas (<code>nums</code>) and <code>h</code> hours (passed as <code>target</code>) before the guards return. Each hour she eats up to <code>k</code> bananas from one pile. Return the minimum integer speed <code>k</code> so she finishes all piles within <code>h</code> hours.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  piles = [3,6,7,11], h = 8
Output: 4</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  piles = [30,11,23,4,20], h = 5
Output: 30</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>1 &lt;= piles.length &lt;= 10^4</li><li>piles.length &lt;= h &lt;= 10^9</li><li>1 &lt;= piles[i] &lt;= 10^9</li></ul>
