<h1>Swap Nodes in Pairs</h1>
<span class="lc-diff medium">Medium</span>
<details class="lc-topics"><summary>Topics</summary><div class="lc-tags"><span class="lc-tag">Linked List</span><span class="lc-tag">Recursion</span></div></details>
<p>Given the <code>head</code> of a linked list, swap every two adjacent nodes and return its head. You must swap the nodes themselves, not just their values. A trailing odd node stays in place.</p>
<h2>Example 1</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3,4]
Output: [2,1,4,3]</pre>
<h2>Example 2</h2><pre>Input:  head = [1,2,3]
Output: [2,1,3]</pre>
<h2>Constraints</h2><ul><li>0 &lt;= number of nodes &lt;= 100</li><li>0 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 100</li></ul>
