Each page lets you **filter by location** (Bengaluru, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, Noida, Remote, and more), **filter by category** (FAANG, Product, Fintech, HFT, Enterprise), set a **minimum comp**, and **sort** any column.

> **How to read this:** Figures are **approximate total compensation** (base + stocks/RSUs + bonus), in INR lakhs per annum (LPA), for **India** locations, aggregated from public sources (**Levels.fyi, AmbitionBox, Glassdoor**). They vary by team, location, and negotiation. **These are estimates, not offers** — always confirm with the recruiter.

### Notes
- **Comp structure:** most product companies pay **base + RSUs (4-yr vesting) + bonus**; stock-heavy offers swing with share price.
- **HFT/quant** (DE Shaw, Tower Research, Graviton) sit at the top and are cash-heavy.
- **Location:** Bengaluru and Hyderabad usually pay most; NCR (Gurgaon/Noida) is strong for Google, Microsoft, Adobe, Amazon, and the Gurgaon HFT firms.

*Sources: Levels.fyi, AmbitionBox, Glassdoor (community-reported, approximate). Last compiled 2026.*
