# Target Companies by Level — India (SDE-1 / SDE-2 / SDE-3)

A shortlist of strong companies to target for software engineering roles in India, grouped by level, with **estimated total-compensation bands**.

> **How to read this:** Figures are **approximate total compensation** (base + stocks/RSUs + bonus), in INR lakhs per annum (LPA), for **India** locations. They are aggregated from public sources like **Levels.fyi, AmbitionBox, and Glassdoor** and vary by team, location, and negotiation. **These are estimates, not offers** — always confirm with the recruiter. Ranges reflect the typical middle of the market; top-of-band offers exist for strong candidates.

**Rough experience mapping:** SDE-1 ≈ 0–2 yrs · SDE-2 ≈ 2–5 yrs · SDE-3 / Senior ≈ 5–8+ yrs. Titles differ by company (e.g., Google L3/L4/L5, Meta E3/E4/E5, Amazon SDE I/II/III).

### Notes
- **Comp structure:** most product companies pay **base + RSUs (vesting over 4 yrs) + joining/annual bonus**. Stock-heavy offers (Google, Meta, Databricks) swing a lot with stock price.
- **HFT/quant** (DE Shaw, Tower Research, Graviton, Quadeye) sit at the very top and are cash-heavy.
- **Location:** Bengaluru and Hyderabad usually pay the most; NCR (Gurgaon/Noida) is strong for Google, Microsoft, Adobe, Amazon, Sprinklr, and the Gurgaon HFT firms.
- **Verify before you invest time:** ask the recruiter for the band early, especially for startups where ranges vary widely.

*Sources: Levels.fyi, AmbitionBox, Glassdoor (community-reported, approximate). Last compiled 2026.*
